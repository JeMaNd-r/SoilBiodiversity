        Soil Regions of the European Union and Adjacent Countries 1 : 5 000 000 

  ___________________________________________________________________________________

  Metadata:   	For displaying the medadata in ArcGIS please use the ISO-Stylesheet.
  ___________________________________________________________________________________

  Geometry:   	Polygon-Shapefile

  Data fields: 	SOILNR - 	Number of the soil region 
				(1000 = undifferentiated Fluvisols
				(2000 = Anthrosols and urban areas)
		SOILNR_TXT - 	Number for the allocation of a colour value using the 
				provided ESRI stylesheet: color_eusr5000.style
  ___________________________________________________________________________________    

  Topographical base map:	Digital Chart of the World (DCW), Environmental 
				Systems Research Institute, Inc., Redlands, USA
  ___________________________________________________________________________________

  Projection: 	EPSG Code 9802; 
		Lambert Conformal Conic with two standard parallels intersecting 
		at 30°N and 60° N, central meridian at 20° E 
		Datum: World Geodetic System 1984 (WGS84)
