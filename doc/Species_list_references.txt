Isopods 
- soil taxa: Straalen 2021, Pedobiologia: terrestrialization for soil
-- terrestrial suborder Oniscidea, ~3,700 species, 4 terrestrial sub(sub)orders: Crinocheta, Sinocheta, Microcheta, and Diplocheta

Tardigrades
- soil taxa: Straalen 2021, Pedobiologia: terrestrialization for soil
-- all terrestrial after Heterotardigrada (Arthrot. & Echiniscoidea), and except at least 62 exclusively freshwater-living species (see Garey et al. 2008)

Insects (Hexapods)
- soil taxa: Straalen 2021, Pedobiologia: terrestrialization for soil
-- mostly terrestrial, many with freshwater-stage
-- fully terrestrial (!not necessarily soil): Hymenoptera (wasps, bees, ants), Dermaptera (earwigs), Apterygota (wingless hexapods), Isoptera (termites), Blattodea (cockroaches), mantodea (praying mantids), Phasmatodea (stick insects), Embioptera (webspinners), Orthoptera (grasshoppers, crickets)

Platyhelminthes
- soil taxa: Straalen 2021, Pedobiologia: terrestrialization for soil
-- order Tricladida: Bipaliidae, Microplaninae, Rhynchodeminae, Caenoplaninae, and Geoplaninae are terrestrial, remaining are aquatic

Snails & slugs
- soil taxa: Straalen 2021, Pedobiologia: terrestrialization for soil
-- land snails, e.g. Stylommatophora (Helix, Limax, Cepaea, Arion), some Systellommatophora (Veronicella) and some Ellobioidae (Carychium)
-- Caenogastopoda: terrestrial taxa: Cyclophoroidae (Leptopoma, Arinia)
